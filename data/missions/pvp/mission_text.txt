以下情况会导致失去同步：
1. 对战双方帧率不相同，建议setting.json使用默认设置："fps":60
2. 在开发者模式下，使用 Ctrl + LMB 获取飞船控制权
3. 使用带有以下系统的飞船
    (1) 相位斗篷
    (2) 时流之壳
    (3) 终结者无人机

The following situations will cause loss of synchronization:
1. The frame rate of both sides of the battle is not the same, it is recommended that setting.json use the default setting: "fps":60
2. Use Ctrl + LMB to get control of the ship in developer mode.
3. Use a ship with the following systems
    (1) Phase Cloak
    (2) Temporal Shell
    (3) Termination Sequence
